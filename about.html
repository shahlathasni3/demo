About Us

Al-Hazeem Kuwait Co. for General Contracting for Buildings

Al-Hazeem Kuwait Co. for General Contracting for Buildings having its registered office in Oman Street, it is a Kuwait Municipality approved General Contracting Company. We have been executing medium scale projects Since inception we have focused our efforts on delivering the highest level of service and quality workmanship through our team of dedicated Engineers and Site Operatives. It is our aim to maintain a manageable level of projects, the focus being on quality rather than quantity, having gained a reputation of delivering the highest quality fit outs, professionally executed, within program and budget constraints.
